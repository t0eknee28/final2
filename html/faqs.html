FAQs

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art and Olympic sport that involves punching, blocking, and kicking techniques

Taekwondo originated in Korea and has roots in martial arts from Japan and China. The word "taekwondo" is a combination of three Korean words that mean "foot," "fist," and "way".

An estimated 80 million people in 209 countries practice taekwondo, making it one of the world's most popular sports.
